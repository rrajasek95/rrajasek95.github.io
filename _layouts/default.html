About Me

Hi, I'm Rishi! I am a 1st year MS in CSE student at UC Santa Cruz. My research interest lies broadly in Natural Language Understanding and Semantic Analysis and hope to work on projects in those areas.


I'm a native of Chennai, one of the many temple cities in India, where I spent the whole of my childhood.


I did my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Trichy in Tamil Nadu. I spent a huge chunk of time over there participating in quizzes, web development and getting cooked in the heat! I was a member of Spider R&D, a technology club, and Balls by Picasso, the literary club.


I joined Morgan Stanley in 2017 where I worked as a Java developer in the Wealth Management Division. My work involved building backend systems for account creation and client onboarding.
